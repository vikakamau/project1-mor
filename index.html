WELCOME TO VIKOZ MOTORS

ABOUT US

We have been importing vehicles from Japan for our customers since 2021. Utilizing the internet, our knowledge of Japanese auctions, and networks with domestic car dealers in Japan, we put thousands of Japanese cars at your fingertips. We then help you buy and import cars directly from Japan to Kenya at affordable prices stress-free. Our service includes locating your ideal vehicle, inspection, shipping, clearance, registration, and delivery to your doorstep. We also offer vehicle customization services depending on your preferences.

This seamless end-to-end service coupled with a comfortable payment schedule allows you to get a quality vehicle, make fantastic savings and avoid the nuisance and stress associated with car importation.

And now, to cater to the growing demand for locally available vehicles, we have opened a showroom along Karen Road, opposite the Waterfront Mall. Whether you are looking to import a car from Japan, purchase a new import already in the country or buy a locally used car, we will do our best to meet your requirements. We also offer local financing options for those funded by a bank, Sacco, or employer, as well as trade-in services.

The saying goes that a change is as good as a rest. Vikoz Motors previously operated as Jap Imports Ltd. We have since fully transitioned, and other than the name, our team and spirit of service remain the same.

We say Karibu Sana to our new showroom in Karen, and we look forward to meeting all your vehicle needs.
